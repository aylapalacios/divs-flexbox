I grew up moving up and down the West Coast developing an appreciation, bordering on reverence, for the natural landscape. The pine trees, mountains, lakes, and dry desert rocks feel like home. I am drawn to anything that allows me to experience a deeper connection to a landscape.
Whether it's a hike, forcing me to experience the landscape only as quickly as my feet can carry me, or an off-road drive over rocks, stumps, and fallen trees, pushing me deeper into the unknown, or sitting quietly in a home that presents the landscape as the main attractions.
Natural landscapes undoubtedly shaped my view of the world. My preference is towards simplicity so as to not disrupt the flow between nature and consciousness. Fewer distractions from the natural order of things. Nature as inspiration. Nature as guide. Less is more. Nature first.
