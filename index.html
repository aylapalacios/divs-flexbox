Hi, I'm Ayla.

I am a Responsive Developer & Designer. I'm a big fan of solving problems (the more creative/weirder, the better). When I'm not working, I love flipping through design books, baking, and spending time in nature.

I grew up moving up and down the West Coast. So, I tend to feel most at home in and around forests, mountains, lakes, and dry desert rocks. Undoubtedly, this has shaped my view of the world.

Whether it's a hike, an off-road drive, or spending time in a built structure that presents the landscape as the main attraction, I tend to draw on natural experiences for inspiration.

Working on web projects, although completely opposite the natural environment, affords the opportunity to take the lessons of nature and transform them into digital applications. Simplicity over, superflusous. Less is more.
